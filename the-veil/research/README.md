# Research

This directory contains peer-reviewed and community-contributed research across multiple domains.

## Research Topics

### Cryptography & Security
Foundational and applied cryptographic research, security protocols, and threat analysis.

### Privacy & Surveillance
Research on privacy-preserving technologies, surveillance systems, and policy implications.

### Decentralization
Distributed systems, peer-to-peer architectures, and alternative organizational models.

### Digital Culture & Ethics
How technology shapes society, digital rights, and ethical frameworks for technology design.

## Contributing Research

1. Create a subdirectory for your topic (e.g., `cryptography/`)
2. Add a `README.md` describing the topic and linking to papers within
3. Write individual research files following the format in [STRUCTURE.md](../../STRUCTURE.md)
4. Include front-matter with title, author, date, and tags
5. Open a PR with your contribution

## Format Template

```markdown
---
title: "Research Title"
author: "Author Name"
date: "YYYY-MM-DD"
tags: ["tag1", "tag2", "tag3"]
status: "draft" # or "published"
---

# Research Title

## Introduction
Briefly introduce the research question or topic.

## Background
Provide context and reference prior work.

## Methodology
Describe your approach or analysis method.

## Findings
Present your results and key insights.

## Implications
Discuss what this means for practice or theory.

## References
- [Citation 1](link)
- [Citation 2](link)
```
